AI in Cybersecurity

AI is reshaping how SOCs detect and respond to threats — processing millions of events per second and surfacing actionable signals from noise. As a future analyst, I actively study how AI augments human decision-making in security operations.

  • Anomaly Detection: ML baselines "normal" behavior and flags deviations in real-time.
  • UEBA: Tracks user behavior patterns to detect account takeovers without known signatures.
  • Threat Intelligence: NLP models parse threat feeds and CVE databases to auto-surface IOCs.
  • SOAR Integration: AI-driven playbooks auto-triage low-severity alerts so analysts focus on what matters.
  • Predictive Defense: AI maps threat actor TTPs to predict campaigns before they hit.
